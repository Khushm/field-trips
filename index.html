Field Trips & Annual Energy Excursions

Exploring Energy in the Real World

Our lab organizes yearly field trips to cutting-edge energy facilities, drilling sites, and sustainability projects. These experiences provide valuable insights into subsurface energy systems and their role in a sustainable future.

2019 – Houston Energy Expo

Students toured major energy facilities and engaged with industry leaders, learning about the latest drilling and sustainability practices.

2020 – Marcellus Shale Visit

A hands-on experience observing shale gas extraction and sustainability techniques in Pennsylvania.
